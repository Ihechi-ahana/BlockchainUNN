Who we are?

We are a community of Digital Enthusiasts with a drive to educate, impact and develop members of the University Community with basic knowledge of cryptocurrency and blockchain education, as well as development in UNN/UNEC Campuses.

Welcome to our Community

BlockchainUNN is a campus blockchain club created at the University of Nigeria aimed at promoting blockchain adoption within the university community. It was founded by Okoye Kevin Chibuoyim, Okechukwu Alvan and Nduka Annaelechukwu Anthony.

Our Journey

History

BlockchainUNN was founded on April 27, 2021 by Nduka Anthony -Blockchain Technology Enthusiast and FTX UNN Campus Ambassador, Okoye Kevin Chibuoyim - CEO & Founder, GinaKev Digital Academy (GIDA) and Okechukwu Alvan Victor - Director, Alvan CryptoHub.

The Genesis

Community Logo

To represent the lion UNN holds in high esteem as well as Blockchain Concept. The logo was scripted from scratch to combine both the lion and Blockchain nodes.

Community Mission and Vision

To develop and educate community members (including students and lecturers) of the University of Nigeria with basic knowledge of Cryptocurrency on Blockchain Education and Development. We have the mision of being the best Campus Blockchain Community in Africa.

First-ever Blockchain UNN Campus Online Meet up

To further spread the Blockchain education, the first webinar kicking off the establishment was held on Sunday, 17th October, 2011. Over 32+ students were present and educated at this webinar

Focus

Our focus is exploring and educating community members on different facets and applications of Blockchain Technology
